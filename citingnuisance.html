Citing NUISANCE

NUISANCE is released under the GPL open source software license and is freely availble for anyone to use.

If you use NUISANCE please cite the following reference:

NUISANCE: a neutrino cross-section generator tuning and comparison framework. P. Stowell, C. Wret, C. Wilkinson, L. Pickering, et. al., 2017 JINST 12 P01016, arXiv:1612.07393

or use this pre-defined latex:

@article{Stowell:2016jfr, author = "Stowell, P. and others", title = "{NUISANCE: a neutrino cross-section generator tuning and comparison framework}", eprint = "1612.07393", archivePrefix = "arXiv", primaryClass = "hep-ex", doi = "10.1088/1748-0221/12/01/P01016", journal = "JINST", volume = "12", number = "01", pages = "P01016", year = "2017" }

or use InspireHEP.

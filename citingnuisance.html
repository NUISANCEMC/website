Citing NUISANCE

NUISANCE is released under the GPL open source software license and is freely availble for anyone to use.

If you use NUISANCE please cite the following reference:

NUISANCE: a neutrino cross-section generator tuning and comparison framework.
P. Stowell, C. Wret, C. Wilkinson, L. Pickering, et. al., 2017 JINST 12 P01016, arXiv:1612.07393
